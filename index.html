STORY
MOBATECH is an independent software provider, technology research and investment. We are delivering open gaming platforms and professional services to both the online and land based gaming sectors.
We believe in value driven technology solutions that simplify your business whether you are an operator, vendor or regulator.
Vision
We strive to become a perfect omni-channel and multi-product gaming environments that attracts many exciting games and tournaments. Build an "explosive" playground for e-sport teams and become the venue for professional sports tournaments in the digital space.
In the long term, MOBATECH's goal is to become the world's top 5 gaming service management enterprise and the first step towards implementing Metaverse for gamers.
Mission
Our mission is to create an equitable playing field for all gamers that engage yours players with superior service and incentives .Help developers and sponsors maximize the benefits of participating in the MOBATECH ecosystem. We will develop a metaverse platform where viewers can watch their favorite tournaments in the most real way.
